## The Problem

A regional HVAC contractor was losing qualified leads to competitors despite
strong service-area demand — the cause was invisible at the surface level.

## The Diagnostic

A Google Business Profile integrity scan revealed unverified service areas,
primary category misalignment, and incomplete attribute coverage throttling
the profile's eligibility for relevant queries.

## The Intervention

Category hierarchy was realigned, service areas were verified, and attribute
coverage was completed against the diagnostic payload.

## The Outcome

Qualified lead flow recovered as the profile became eligible for the full
range of high-intent local searches.
