## The Problem

A multi-location dental group presented inconsistent NAP data across local
directories, fragmenting their Google Business Profile authority and
suppressing map-pack visibility.

## The Diagnostic

The SEO Janitor automation aggregated citation consistency, profile integrity,
and on-page metadata anomalies across all 11 locations, surfacing 47 distinct
inconsistencies in a single pass.

## The Intervention

Findings were compiled into a prioritized remediation payload. Citation
records were reconciled and structured data was normalized across the network.

## The Outcome

Map-pack visibility was restored within one audit cycle, with measurable
improvement in local query coverage.
